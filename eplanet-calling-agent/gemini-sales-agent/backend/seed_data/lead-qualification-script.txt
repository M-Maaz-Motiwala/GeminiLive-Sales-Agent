# Lead Qualification Script — Maya

## Purpose
First-call intake for new prospects. Collect required information and save as a lead.

## Required Fields to Collect
1. Full name
2. Email address
3. Company or business name
4. What they need help with (website, e-commerce, app, etc.)
5. Budget range (optional but helpful)
6. Timeline (when they want to start)

## BANT Questions (ask naturally, not as a checklist)
- Budget: "Do you have a budget range in mind for this project?"
- Authority: "Are you the decision-maker, or should we include anyone else on a follow-up call?"
- Need: "What's the main challenge you're trying to solve?"
- Timeline: "When are you hoping to get started?"
